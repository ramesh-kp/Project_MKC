Hardware connection
1.	Connect the 12 v power supply to the water level sensor.
	The red wire (positive) to white wire of the water level sensor.
	The black wire (negative) to brown wire of the water level sensor.
2.	Connect the RS-485 connection of water level sensor to the ethernet interface.
	The green wire (RS485+) of water level sensor to the green wire of the ethernet cable.
	The yellow wire (RS485-) of water level sensor to the brown-white wire of the ethernet cable.
3.	Connect the ethernet cable to the port of the 16-port gateway hub.
	150 - Com 1 of the 16 port gateway hub.
